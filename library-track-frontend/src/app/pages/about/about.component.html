About The Grand Library

A century of knowledge, community, and heritage at the heart of the city.

📖 Our Story

Preserving History, Inspiring Future

Founded in 1895, The Grand Library has been a cornerstone of the community for over a century. What began as a modest reading room of 500 volumes has grown into one of the region's most beloved cultural institutions, housing over 50,000 physical books across dozens of genres and disciplines.

We are dedicated to fostering a love for reading, providing equal access to knowledge, and creating a welcoming space where everyone is invited to learn, grow, and connect.
